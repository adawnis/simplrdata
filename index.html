FAQ

Questions we get all the time.

Do I need any technical knowledge to use the dashboard?
Not at all. We build everything to be as simple as opening Instagram on your phone. If you can scroll and tap, you can use your dashboard. We also provide a full walkthrough session so you and your team feel confident before we hand over.
How do I get my sales data into the dashboard?
We set up a simple template — usually a Google Sheet or an Excel file — where your staff record sales the same way they always have. The dashboard connects to that file and updates automatically. No complicated software, no new apps to learn.
Can I see the dashboard on my phone?
Yes. Your dashboard is accessible from any phone, tablet, or laptop with an internet connection. We optimise it for mobile so you can check your numbers from anywhere in Lagos — or outside it.
How long does it take to build my dashboard?
Typically 3–5 working days from the time we receive your data. For more complex builds with multiple data sources, it can take up to 7 days. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.
What if my business doesn't have organised data yet?
That's actually where most of our clients start. We help you build a simple data collection system first — even just a structured spreadsheet — and then build the dashboard around that. You don't need to have everything sorted before reaching out.
Is my business data safe and private?
Absolutely. Your data belongs to you. We work within your own Google or Microsoft accounts, so no business data sits on our servers. We also sign a confidentiality agreement before starting any engagement.
What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with any product or service business that has data worth tracking. Our clients include retail shops, supermarkets, pharmacies, restaurants, logistics companies, and e-commerce stores. If your business generates transactions, we can build you a dashboard.
What happens after the retainer period ends?
The dashboard is yours to keep — always. At the end of any retainer period, you can continue the subscription, move to a lighter support plan, or manage it yourself. We'll make sure you know how to use it before we step back.
